Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 28, 2025

Our Privacy Commitment

AdvisorNote is built on a foundation of complete privacy. Unlike cloud-based transcription services, we process everything locally on your device, which means we never see, collect, or store your data.

What We Don't Collect

  • • Your audio recordings or transcriptions
  • • Client information or personal data
  • • Usage data or telemetry
  • • Analytics or tracking information
  • • Any personally identifiable information

100% On-Device Processing

All speech recognition happens entirely on your computer. Your audio never leaves your device, is never transmitted over the internet, and is never stored anywhere except your local machine.

Information We May Collect

We only collect minimal information necessary for licensing and support:

  • • Email address (for license delivery and support)
  • • Payment information (processed securely by our payment provider)
  • • Support inquiries (only when you contact us)

Australian Data Sovereignty

AdvisorNote is built for Australian financial professionals. Any data we do collect (email for licensing) is stored in accordance with Australian privacy laws and the Privacy Act 1988.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • • Request deletion of your email and account information
  • • Access any information we hold about you
  • • Opt out of marketing communications

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@advisornote.com
